Terms & Conditions of Service

Device submit karne se pehle कृपया ye points dhyaan se padhein. Job sheet par sign karne ka matlab hai ki aap in terms se सहमत hain.

1. Warranty & Brand Policy

Saare repairs respective manufacturer ke official policy ke hisaab se kiye jaate hain.

  1. Odyssey Technologies (Motorola Authorized Service Center) Motorola ki Manufacturer Limited Warranty (MLW) ke rules follow karta hai.
  2. Xiaomi devices ka service Mi Authorized Service guidelines aur OEM warranty policy ke mutabik hota hai.
  3. Warranty eligibility hamesha technical verification ke baad decide hoti hai (liquid / physical damage ya unauthorized repair check kiya jaata hai). Proof of purchase mangi ja sakti hai.

2. Data, Backup & Screen Protectors

  1. Service center kisi bhi data loss (photos, contacts, apps, files, etc.) ke liye responsible nahi hoga. Device submit karne se pehle complete backup lena customer ki responsibility hai. Service ke dauraan software update, format ya part change ki wajah se data erase ho sakta hai.
  2. Screen protector / tempered glass / skins diagnosis ya repair ke dauraan remove karne pad sakte hain. Inka damage hone par service center liable nahi hoga.

3. Job Sheet, Handover & Collection

  1. Job sheet par customer ke sign ke bina koi bhi device accept nahi kiya jaayega.
  2. Device sirf original signed job sheet copy return karne par hi handover kiya jaayega. Special cases me valid ID & verification mangi ja sakti hai.
  3. Final estimate agar communication ke 7 din ke andar approve nahi hota, to device "as-is" condition me collect karna hoga (agar applicable hua to diagnostic / minimal service charges lag sakte hain).
  4. Jab written intimation (SMS / call / WhatsApp ya physical intimation) diya jaata hai ki device ready hai, to customer ko 14 din ke andar device collect karna hoga.
  5. Written intimation ke 45 din ke andar device collect na karne par, device ko auction / dispose karke service & storage charges recover kiye jaa sakte hain. Aise case me data / accessories recover nahi ho paayenge.

4. Estimates, Charges & Service Warranty

  1. Initial estimate provisional hota hai. Detailed inspection ke baad ya additional fault milne par ye change ho sakta hai. Revised estimate customer ko inform karke hi proceed kiya jaayega.
  2. Non-warranty repairs ka amount OEM ke recommended pricing ke mutabik hota hai, aur applicable taxes (GST etc.) alag se lag sakte hain.
  3. Non-warranty devices par ki gayi repair par aam taur par 30 days service warranty milti hai, jo sirf same issue & same part ke liye valid hai. Isme koi bhi liquid / physical / accidental damage ya delivery ke baad ki tampering cover nahi hoti.
  4. Normal service turnaround time lagbhag 10 din hota hai, lekin parts shortage, logistics, OEM approval ya complex technical issue ki wajah se ye time badh bhi sakta hai.

5. Liability, Force Majeure & Disputes

  1. Natural calamity, strike, lockdown, emergency, server / network outage ya kisi bhi force majeure reason se hone wali delay ke liye service center responsible nahi hoga.
  2. Agar koi dispute mutually resolve nahi hota, to wo arbitration ke through handle kiya jaayega, jisme arbitrator service center dwara good faith me appoint kiya jaayega, as per applicable law.
  3. Ye terms pehle diye gaye saare verbal ya written communication ko supersede karte hain, jab tak job sheet ya official document par specifically kuch aur mention na ho.

6. Customer Declarations & Communication

  1. Customer yeh declare karta hai ki di gayi saari information (naam, contact, device details, purchase proof, issue description, etc.) sahi hai.
  2. Customer service center ko authorize karta hai ki wo unke registered number par call, SMS aur WhatsApp ke through service updates, approvals aur feedback ke liye contact kare.
  3. Job sheet par sign karke customer confirm karta hai ki usne upar diye gaye saare Terms & Conditions padhe, samjhe aur accept kiye hain.

Note: Brand ke special campaigns, extended warranty ya OEM offers (agar koi ho) unke official SMS / email / invoice terms ke mutabik honge. Yahan diye gaye terms general service process ke liye hain.
